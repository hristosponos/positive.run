About Us

Our Running Community

positive.run is worldwide community of runners focused on positive running.

Our Running Community supports positive well-being and creates healthier, happier attitude to life!

It doesn’t matter if you are just a beginner or experienced runner — all runners are welcome to join our community!

We have Running Events every week, we organize Running Camps in different countries and organize Running Races in Estonia and Baltic states.

All our events, running camps and races are organized by a group of positive, dedicated local runners.

We want as many people as possible to feel part of our positive community and welcoming to join our events, running camps and races whenever you want, wherever you like!
